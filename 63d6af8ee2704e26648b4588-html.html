Mbappe's ex-girlfriend catches his eye with viral photo: Looking to win Kylian back?

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kylian Mbappe's relationship with transgender model Ines Rau ended a few weeks ago and he has since been linked to Belgian model Stephanie Rose Bertram.

The French striker has never publicly declared any romantic relationship with either Ines Rau or Rose Bertram, but the tabloids have taken it upon themselves to ignite these stories.

Rau, who was the first trans woman to be on the cover of Playboy, shared a photo on her Instagram account that set social media alight as she posed inside a vehicle in a red evening gown to share that she was on her way to a fashion show, an image that generated thousands of likes and comments.

Some users did not miss the opportunity to link the photo with an intention of Ines Rau to attract the attention of Kylian Mbappe with this seductive post.

Who is Ines Rau?

Ines Rau is a transgender model who has been in the spotlight for a few months now, after images surfaced of her in the arms of Kylian Mbappe.

Ines was born on March 18, 1990, in France and underwent gender reassignment surgery at the age of 16, having been inspired by the story of English trans model Caroline "Tula" Cossey.

According to Elle, Ines Rau previously went by the name Mario Carrizo.

She subsequently lived as a woman but did not disclose her transgender identity until she was 24.

Ines rose to fame after appearing in an episode of TF1's documentary series Sept a Huit, which focuses on a day in the life of a trans model.

She was Playboy magazine's November 2017 Playmate of the Month and the first openly transgender Playmate.

According to the website allfamousbirthday, Ines Rau is one of the richest models and is on the list of most popular models, and her net worth is $5 million.
