Most Awaited Love Journey

The former teacher, Steve Janowitz, started dating his future wife, Joy Behar, in 1982. At the time, Joy had just gotten out of her previous marriage. In March 2009, Joy announced that she would marry Janowitz, but three months later, she called off the engagement.

The couple went on to marry after 29-years of relationship. So, why they waited so long? As per Joy, they had been waiting for same-sex marriage to be legal in New York.

The longtime lovers eventually walked down their wedding aisle on August 11, 2011. Their marriage ceremony was held in an intimate ceremony in New York. As of early 2020, the couple is living a joyful life together.
