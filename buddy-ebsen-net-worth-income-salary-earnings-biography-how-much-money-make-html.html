Buddy Ebsen, also known as Christian Ludolf Ebsen, was an American character actor and dancer who lived from April 2, 1908 until July 6, 200He had big roles as Jed Clampett in the long-running CBS television series The Beverly Hillbillies and as the titular character in the 1970s detective series Barnaby Jones. He was a performer for seven decades. Ebsen also starred as Fess Parker’s sidekick in Walt Disney’s Davy Crockett miniseries (1953–54) and was cast as the Tin Man in The Wizard of Oz (1939) until he became unwell due to a cosmetic allergy. Not as Jed Clampett, but as his other renowned character Barnaby Jones, Ebsen made a cameo appearance in the 1993 film adaptation of The Beverly Hillbillies.
