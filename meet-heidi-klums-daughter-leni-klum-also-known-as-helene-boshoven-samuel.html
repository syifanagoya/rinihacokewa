Leni Klum, also known as Helene Boshoven Samuel, is the daughter of Heidi Klum, the German model, television host, producer, and businesswoman. Her father is Seal, a singer-songwriter. Leni was born on May 4, 2004, and was originally thrown into the limelight because of the celebrity status of her parents. She is currently 19 years old.

Because her parents are global superstars, Leni has also attracted a lot of attention from the media which keeps trying to keep a tab on all that she does with her life.

Leni practically grew up in front of the world as all eyes were on her considering who her parents were. However, even though she is more known as the daughter of Heidi Klum and Seal, Leni has also begun to build her own career in the world of fashion and modeling. In fact, she is now making a name by herself and is currently a fast-rising superstar model who is admired by many. Over the years, she has walked runways and is slowly becoming her own woman.

How Old is Leni Klum?

Meet Leni’s Mother

Apparently, Leni Klum is blessed to have such amazing parents who have done so well for themselves. Their huge popularity has rubbed off her and has thrown her into the public eye. As the years progressed, the media became more interested in her and so, Leni practically grew up in front of the world earning her fair share of fame in the process.

Is Seal Leni Klum’s Biological Father?

Today, the world knows Seal to be the father of Leni Klum whom he loves dearly. However, the interesting thing is that the singer is not actually her biological father. So, how did Seal come to be known as Leni Klum’s father? The story is quite an interesting one.

Leni Klum’s biological father is actually a man called Flavio Briatore, a popular Italian businessman and former Renault Formula One managing director. Briatore and Leni’s mother began dating in March 2003 and were head over heels in love with each other. Soon after they started dating, Heidi became pregnant with Leni and announced her pregnancy in December 2003. However, things took a sad turn between the lovers and the two broke up soon after the pregnancy was announced.

After Heidi and Briatore broke up, the model almost immediately started dating Seal. Seal, who was really in love with Heidi, took care of the model with her pregnancy and she eventually gave birth to Leni Klum on May 4, 2004. According to reports, Seal was right there to witness the birth of Leni and ensured that Heidi was in good shape. Also, Seal has always been there for Leni ever since.

Even though Briatore is the biological father of Leni, Heidi Klum has always insisted that the Italian businessman has never been involved in Leni’s life. She has also stated emphatically that “Seal is Leni’s father.” This is especially because of how actively involved Seal has been in Leni’s life since her birth. In December 2004, several months after Leni’s birth, Heidi Klum and Seal got engaged. A few more months later on 10 May 2005, the couple got married on a beach in Mexico to the excitement of their family and friends.

On the 21st of November 2009, when Leni was about 5 years, her mother Heidi Klum officially adopted Seal’s surname and became legally known as Heidi Samuel. Some weeks after this, in December 2009, Seal officially adopted Leni as his daughter, and, thus, her last name was changed to Samuel. Of course, this was with the permission of Flavio Briatore.

Who are Leni Klum’s Grandparents?

Leni’s legal grandparents on her father’s (Seal) side are Adebisi Ogundeji, a Nigerian woman, and Francis Samuel, an Afro-Brazilian man. On her mother’s side, her grandparents are Erna Klum (née Berger), a hairdresser, and Günther Klum, a cosmetics company executive.

Leni Klum’s Parents are No Longer Together

Sadly, Leni’s parents Heidi Klum and Seal are no more together. The couple has since divorced and parted ways. While they were married, Heidi Klum and Seal were very fond of each other and renewed their vows to one another every year on their anniversary in front of family and friends. It appeared that they were going to last forever as a couple.

However, things took a dramatic and sad turn on 22 January 2012, when the couple shocked fans by announcing that they were separating after almost seven years of marriage. The development left many people startled because the couple had appeared to be a perfect fit. Eventually, Heidi Klum filed for divorce from Seal on 6 April 2012 and requested that her name be restored to her birth name. Sadly, the divorce was finalized on 14 October 2014.

In the statement they released about the matter, the couple explained that the divorce was an amicable one and that there is no bad blood between them as they both had the greatest respect for one another. They revealed that the well-being of their kids was their biggest priority. However, even though the couple claimed they were splitting amicably, reports began to emerge that Klum and Seal battled a number of issues before the marriage eventually collapsed.

Reports claimed that Heidi Klum decided to divorce because of Seal’s hot temper. It was claimed that Klum was worried about Seal’s inability to control his temper and the effect it was having on their children. There were also reports that Seal was partying way too hard and Klum was fed up with this. Their very hectic work schedules also pulled them apart and everything came crashing down.

Despite their split, the former couple is working hard to be awesome co-parents to their kids. Leni is especially very fond of Seal and has been pictured with him on different occasions. In October 2021, Leni made a red carpet appearance with Seal at a screening of the Netflix film “The Harder They Fall”. The pair looked simply dashing and photos of both of them went viral.

Information about the elementary or high schools that Leni Klum attended remains quite vague at the moment. However, in 2020, the model revealed that during the lockdown due to the coronavirus outbreak, she had to change to different schools and only knew most of her classmates via zoom.

Leni Klum is Building an Amazing Modeling Career

Leni Klum is taking after her mother and is slowly rising to become one of the highly sought-after supermodels in the world. She started taking interest in modeling when she was very little especially because she kept watching her mother go on modeling trips and get involved in fashion shows and events. She imagined herself cat-walking on runways and appearing on magazine covers like her mother.

Even though she was still just a child, Leni began to carry herself like a model, just like her mother, and this glaring fact was not lost on agencies. Soon, when she was about 12 or 13 years old, Leni revealed that she got her first-ever modeling offer from Brandy Melville, a fashion company she loves. According to her, she was shopping with her mother at the time and so excited about the deal that really wanted to take the offer but her mother refused, explaining that Leni was too young to go into modeling and that she had to wait till she was at least 16.

Leni’s love for modeling was heightened even more some years later when she accompanied her mother to the set of Germany’s Next Topmodel, a German modeling/fashion TV show. Leni’s mom was the host of the show and sits on the judge’s panel so it was easy for her to bring Leni along. While the show was going on, Leni saw contestants being challenged to take photos mid-air while they jumped off a building. This caught her interest so she asked her mother if she could also take part in jumping and having her photo taken and her mother agreed.

The experience was incredible for Leni who felt, at that very moment, that modeling was what she wanted to do with her life. Her mother Heidi also revealed, once, that her daughter loved coming to the set of the modeling show to just see models do what they had to do and learn from them. It was clear to everyone that Leni was completely interested in becoming a model and was ready to take on the role.

Who has Leni Klum Modeled for?

Leni Klum has modeled for several big brands, including Vogue, among others. She made her modeling debut in December 2020 by posing for the January/February 2021 issue of Vogue Germany alongside her mother, Heidi Klum. Leni was 16 years old at the time this happened. She was ecstatic! It was no small feat for anyone to make their debut as a model by posing for a magazine as prestigious as Vogue Germany.

In the cover photo, Heidi Klum is seen gently planting a smooch on her teen daughter’s cheek while Leni looks into the camera with her hands confidently in her pocket. In another photo, the mother rests her head on her daughter’s shoulder. Heidi Klum was extremely proud of her daughter’s achievement and proudly made this known on her social media pages. She expressed her immense joy at her daughter’s incredible start to modeling as well as her anticipation that Leni would enjoy a great modeling career.

Leni Klum has since gone ahead to do other great things. In January 2021, the model made her runway debut during Berlin Fall/Winter 2021 Fashion Week, stunning many onlookers with her confidence and charm. At the event, Leni appeared in a digital presentation promoting several designers, including Kaviar Gauche and Michael Sontag.

Some months later, in April 2021, Leni Klum followed in her supermodel mother’s footsteps by landing her first solo magazine cover on Glamour Germany. It is noteworthy that Leni’s mother, Heidi had graced the same magazine cover about 20 years earlier during the publication’s inaugural issue in 2001. Heidi was very proud of her daughter and quickly expressed this on Instagram by sharing the photo online. In the picture, Leni Klum is seen striking a pose in a floral-print dress complete with whimsical sleeves.

A few more months later, in August 2021, Leni Klum returned to the runway and walked the catwalk at the Dolce & Gabbana Alta Moda fashion show in Venice, Italy. Leni found a lot of support there because her mother and other family members also joined her for the trip and sat front-row at the show to watch and cheer her. The same month, Leni wowed in a cover shoot for Rollacoaster magazine.

On September 11, 2021, Leni, who was already 17 years old at the time, walked the runway at German online retailer About You’s Berlin Fashion Week show. Like she did before, Leni also wowed the audience with her grace and charm. This time, she also appeared as a designer and actually showcased designs from her own branded collection. It was clear to all that, Leni was becoming a superstar top model in her own right.

In December 2021, Leni Klum and her mother Heidi both featured on the cover page of Elle Rusia. The elated Leni quickly shared the photo on her Instagram page, expressing her love for her mother.

In the same month of December 2021, Leni featured alone in the international fashion magazine, Numero Russia. Leni has also posed for other magazines and outfits including Elle USA, Creative Artists Agency, Chopard, and others.

Leni Klum is More Than Just a Model

Because of what she has already achieved so far, it is expected that Leni Klum will continue doing exploits in the world of modeling and fashion just like her mother has done, and maybe even surpass the older Klum in the process. However, modeling is not the only thing she does with herself. She has been able to channel her creative energy into other ventures.

We found out that the model is actually an avid dancer and has even trained with Abby Lee Miller, the popular American dance instructor, choreographer, reality television personality. According to Leni, her experience training as a dancer with Abby Lee Miller is what has helped her lose her stage fright when she is on the runway.

Leni Klum is also a budding environmentalist. In fact, the young woman has started a project with Plant-for-the-Planet called Klum Forest to plant trees in Mexico’s Yucatán Peninsula. This very bold move has been hailed by many people who see Leni as one of the young people who would change the world with her deep concern about protecting the environment as time goes on.

Also, Leni is interested in fashion design. The young woman wants to become a powerhouse in fashion design and create lines that would become globally famous. Leni hopes to attend fashion or art school in New York and become better equipped with all she needs to achieve her dreams. Already, the model has created a clothing line for About You, a German e-commerce site. The clothing line actually opened Berlin Fashion Week where Leni and her friends modeled. Apparently, things are already looking very good for Leni.
