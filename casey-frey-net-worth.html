The Viral Sensation

Casey Frey, born on June 23, 1993, in Mendocino County, California, has become an internet sensation known for his comedic sketches, dance videos, and unique brand of humor.

Frey’s journey into the spotlight began with Vine, where his creative and often surreal videos quickly amassed a large following.

His early work, characterized by its originality and Frey’s charismatic delivery, laid the groundwork for a career that would soon expand beyond short-form videos.

From Vine to Viral Stardom

With the closure of Vine, Frey didn’t miss a beat, transitioning his content to platforms like Instagram and YouTube, where his audience continued to grow.

His 2019 viral dance video to GOMF by Dvbbs showcased not only his dance skills but also his ability to create content that resonates with a wide audience.

This video, among others, has been instrumental in establishing Frey as a viral star, leading to opportunities in music videos and collaborations with artists like Donald Glover, Marshmello, Tiesto, and DVBBS.
